# Balaile Tech Lab - Project History

## Project Overview

Balaile Tech Lab is a long-term technology journey focused on developing
engineering skills, building practical digital solutions, and creating
technology products that solve real-world problems.

The project is designed to transform the founder from a traditional
computer science graduate into a Product Engineer and Tech Founder capable
of designing, developing, deploying, and maintaining technology solutions.
