# Balaile Tech Lab - Learning Roadmap

## Purpose

This document defines the learning journey, technical growth path,
and skill development strategy for Balaile Tech Lab.

The goal is to develop the mindset and capability of a Product Engineer
who can design, build, deploy, maintain, and improve technology solutions.

---

# Learning Philosophy

Balaile Tech Lab follows this engineering cycle:

Learn
↓
Practice
↓
Build
↓
Document
↓
Improve
↓
Create Impact

Learning is not considered complete until knowledge is applied through
practical projects.

---
